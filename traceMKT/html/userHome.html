NECO 2022 SSCE EXTERNAL RESULTS NOW AVAILABLE ONLINE: STUDENTS CAN EASILY ACCESS THEIR SCORES

The National Examinations Council (NECO) has recently released the results of the 2022 Senior School Certificate Examination (SSCE) External online. This is great news for the thousands of students who took part in the exam and have been eagerly awaiting their results.
